Hussain Sagar Lake

About:

Hussain Sagar Lake Lord buddha statue is also one of the global icon of Hyderabad. The lake spread over an area of 5.7kms and the lord buddha consists of 450tons with an height of 18m. It was built in the year 1562 and the lord buddha statue was erected in the year of 1992.
