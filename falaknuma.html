Taj Falaknuma Palace

About:

The word Falak-numa in Urdu means ‘Mirror of the Sky’.This palace was built by Nawab Viqar-Ul-Umra, who was the Prime Minister of Hyderabad. it took 9 years to build it. Spread over an area of 32 acres on a 2000-ft high hilltop, the palace is a unique architecture with a harmonious blend of Italian and Tudor styles. Until 2000, the palace was not opened to the public as it was a private property of the Nizams.It was in the year 2000, Taj group of hotels took over the renovation and restoring the palace and it was reopened in 2010.
